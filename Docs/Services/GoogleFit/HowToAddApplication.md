# GoogleFit registration  
Added 02.08.2018.

1. To register an account, follow the link (https://www.google.ru/).

2. In the window that opens, click on the "Sign in" button.

![image001](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image001.jpg)

3. Then click on the "Create account" link.

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image002.jpg)

4. Fill in the required data and click on the "Next" button.

![image003](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image003.jpg)

5. In the next window, specify the phone number, date of birth, gender and click on the "Next" button.

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image004.jpg)

6. It is necessary to verify your phone number by clicking on the "Send" button.

![image005](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image005.jpg)

7. Specify the code that came in the SMS, and click "Verify".

![image006](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image006.jpg)

8. Read the license agreement and click on the "I agree" button.

![image007](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image007.jpg)

9. You have signed up for Google.

![image008](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image008.jpg)

10. To register the application, follow the link (https://console.developers.google.com/).

11. Click the "Create Project" button.

![image009](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image009.jpg)

12. In the next window, click on the "Create" button.

![image010](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image010.jpg)

13. Specify the name of the project and click on the "Create" button.

![image011](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image011.jpg)

14. Go to the "Library" category.

![image012](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image012.jpg)

15. Here you need to select the service you are interested in.

![image013](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image013.jpg)

16. On the next page, turn on the API by clicking on the "Enable" button.

![image014](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image014.jpg)

17. Next you will be redirected to the settings window of the selected service, click there "Create credentials" button.

![image015](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image015.jpg)

18. Make the initial settings and click on "What credentials do i need?".

![image016](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image016.jpg)

19. In the next window, specify Redirect URL and click on the "Create OAuth client ID" button.

![image017](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image017.jpg)

20. In the next window, specify «Product name shown to users» and click the «Continue» button.

![image018](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image018.jpg)

21. Now you can download the required Client ID and Client Secret by clicking on the "Download" button, then click on the "Done" button.

![image019](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image019.jpg)

22. The settings have been made.

![image020](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleFit/image020.jpg)
