# PredictBGL registration  
Added 07.08.2018.

1. Go to (https://predictbgl.com/)
and press “SignUp” button.

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/PredictBGL/image002.gif)

2. Write e-mail and press “START NOW”.

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/PredictBGL/image004.gif)

3. Fill the fields and press “DONE”.

![image006](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/PredictBGL/image006.gif)

4. After registration will be seen next page.

![image008](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/PredictBGL/image008.gif)

5. Go to e-mail which was sent with a message.

![image010](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/PredictBGL/image010.gif)

6. Click on “activation” link, then your browser will be redirected to next page.

![image012](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/PredictBGL/image012.gif)

Press “LOGIN TO THE WEBSITE” button and authorize.

7. Press on “Settings” button.

![image014](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/PredictBGL/image014.gif)

8. In the next page find “Sharing and APIs”.  
Copy your Api_key to your APP.

![image016](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/PredictBGL/image016.gif)

Good Luck!
