¹ VK registration

Added 26 april 2018.

1. To register your application, follow next steps  .
Use the link: (https://vk.com/dev).  
Click the “My apps” button.

![image001](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/VKontakte/image001.png)


2. Log in.

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/VKontakte/image002.png)

3. Click the “Create application” button.

![image003](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/VKontakte/image003.png)

4. Choose the Website platform, fill in all the required fields and click the “Connect Site” button.

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/VKontakte/image004.png)

5. In the application settings you need to fill out authorized redirect URL.  
Now you can get the required for bNesis “Application ID” and “Secure key”.

![image005](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/VKontakte/image005.png)
