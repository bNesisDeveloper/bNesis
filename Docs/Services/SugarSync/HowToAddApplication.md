¹ SugarSync registration

1 To register an account, follow the link (https://www.sugarsync.com).

2. Click on the "Log In".

![image001](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image001.png)

3. Click on the link “Sign up now!”.

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image002.png)

4. Fill in the form and click on the "Limited 5 GB Plan" button.

![image003](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image003.png)

5. Agree to the terms and click on "Download Now".

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image004.png)

6. You have downloaded the program "SugarSync". Click on the installation file and install this program.

![image005](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image005.png)

7. Run the installed program and in the open window click on the "Sign Up" button.

![image006](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image006.png)

8. Fill out the necessary data and click on the "Sign Up".

![image007](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image007.png)

9. Answer the question and click on "OK".

![image008](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image008.png)

10. You are registered.

![image009](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image009.png)

11. Now you need to verify your email. In the mail client, find the email sent to you and click on the "Get Started Now" button.

![image010](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image010.png)

12. After that you will be redirected to the page where you will need to enter your registration data and click on the "Log In" button.

![image011](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image011.png)

13. Verification and registration of the account was successfully completed.

![image012](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image012.png)

14. To register the application, go to (https://www.sugarsync.com/developer/account).

15. Enter your details for the account and click on "Log in".

![image013](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image013.png)

16. Then click on the "Create App" button.

![image014](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image014.png)

17. Fill in the data in the opened window and click on the "Create App" button.

![image015](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image015.png)

18. In the next window you can get the data necessary for authorization in SugarSync.

![image016](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarSync/image016.png)
