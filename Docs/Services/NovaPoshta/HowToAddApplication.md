# NovaPoshta registration

Created on 06/19/2018.

To receive a key in NovaPoshta service you need to register and enter your personal account.

1. To register an account, click on the link (https://novaposhta.ua/) and click on the "Register" button.

![image001](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/NovaPoshta/image001.jpg)

2. Enter all the necessary data for registration and click on the "Register" button.

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/NovaPoshta/image002.jpg)

3. Then, after confirming the phone number and e-mail, go to the login page in your personal account.

![image003](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/NovaPoshta/image003.jpg)

4. In the personal account, go to the "Settings" tab.

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/NovaPoshta/image004.jpg)

5. Next, go to the "API 2.0" tab.

![image005](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/NovaPoshta/image005.jpg)

6. We click on the button «Create a new key» and get the key that we use as ClientId when authorizing the service.

![image006](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/NovaPoshta/image006.jpg)
