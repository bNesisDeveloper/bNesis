# Clarifai registration  
Added 10.08.2018.

1. Go to (https://clarifai.com/)  
and press “LOG IN” button.

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Clarifai/image002.gif)

2. In next page press the link “Sign up now”.

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Clarifai/image004.gif)

3. Fill the all required fields.

![image006](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Clarifai/image006.gif)

4. Press “SIGN UP” button.

![image008](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Clarifai/image008.gif)

5. You’ve been referred to next page.
Note. For getting “Community Plan” you must verify your e-mail address.

![image010](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Clarifai/image010.gif)

6. Go to your account, then select applications and press “CREATE NEW APPLICATION” button.

![image012](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Clarifai/image012.gif)

7. Fill a field “APP NAME” and press “CREATE APP” button.

![image014](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Clarifai/image014.gif)

8. After APP creating go to “API Keys”.

![image016](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Clarifai/image016.gif)

9. Copy your API Key to your App.

![image018](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Clarifai/image018.gif)

Good Luck!
