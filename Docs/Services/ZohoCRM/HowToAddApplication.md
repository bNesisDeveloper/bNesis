Zoho Registration

1. Go to: (https://www.zoho.com/).

2. Click: SIGN UP NOW.

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/ZohoCRM/image002.gif)

3. Fill the registration form.

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/ZohoCRM/image004.gif)

4. Check: “ I agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy”.

![image006](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/ZohoCRM/image006.jpg)

5. Press “SIGN UP FOR FREE”.

![image008](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/ZohoCRM/image008.jpg)

6. Wait while account creating.

![image010](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/ZohoCRM/image010.jpg)

7. After registration, you’ve been redirected to (https://home.zoho.com/home).

![image012](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/ZohoCRM/image012.gif)

8. At now, Go to (https://accounts.zoho.com/developerconsole).

![image014](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/ZohoCRM/image014.gif)

9. Verify your e-mail, if you don’t see “Creating identificator client”.

![image016](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/ZohoCRM/image016.jpg)

10. After verifying e-mail click on “Creating identificator client”.

![image018](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/ZohoCRM/image018.gif)

11. Fill the form. 

![image020](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/ZohoCRM/image020.jpg)

12. Click on “Create”.

![image022](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/ZohoCRM/image022.jpg)

13. Copy client ID & Secret to your app.

![image024](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/ZohoCRM/image024.gif)
