To use the API of Xero you will need to generate API credentials.

1. Go to the page (https://www.xero.com/signup/api/), enter information about you and click ‘Sign Up’.

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Xero/image002.jpg)

2. You need to verify your e-mail.

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Xero/image004.jpg)

3. You have to enter your new password for service and phone and click ‘Activate Your Account’.

![image006](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Xero/image006.jpg)

4. You have to go to the Xero developers page (https://developer.xero.com/myapps) and click ‘New App’.

![image008](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Xero/image008.jpg)

5. You have to choose ‘Public App’, enter information about your App and click ‘Create App’.

![image010](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Xero/image010.jpg)

6. Your API credentials.

![image012](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Xero/image012.jpg)
