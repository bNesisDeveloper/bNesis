# Google Analytics registration

Added May 05, 2018.

1. To register an account, please follow the link (https://www.google.ru/).

2. In the window that opens, click on the button “Sign in”.

![image001](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image001.png)

3. Then click on the link “Create account”.

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image002.png)

4. Fill in the required data and click on "Next".

![image003](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image003.png)

5. In the next window, specify the phone number, date of birth, gender and click on "Next".

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image004.png)

6. It is necessary to verify your phone number, click on the "Send" button.

![image005](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image005.png)

7. Specify the code that came in the SMS, and click on the "Verify".

![image006](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image006.png)

8. Read the license agreement and click on "I agree".

![image007](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image007.png)

9. You signed up for Google.

![image008](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image008.png)

10. To register the application, follow the link
(https://console.developers.google.com/).

11. Click on "Create Project".

![image009](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image009.png)

12. In the next window click on "Create".

![image010](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image010.png)

13. Specify the name of the project and click on "Create".

![image011](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image011.png)

14. Go to the category "Library".

![image012](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image012.png)

15. Here you need to select the service you are interested in.

![image013](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image013.png)

16. On the next page, connect the API by clicking on the "Enable".

![image014](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image014.png)

17. Next you will be redirected to the settings window of the selected service, click on the "Create credentials" button.

![image015](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image015.png)

18. Make the initial settings and click on "What credentials do i need?"

![image016](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image016.png)

19. In the next window, specify the redirect URL and click on the "Create OAuth client ID".

![image017](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image017.png)

20. In the next window, specify "Product name shown to users" and click on "Continue".

![image018](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image018.png)

21. Now you can download the required Client ID and Client Secret by clicking on the "Download" button. After that, click on the "Done" button.

![image019](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image019.png)

22. Settings are made.

![image020](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/GoogleAnalytics/image020.png)
