# SugarCRM registration  
Added on 04.10.2018.

1. Go to (https://www.sugarcrm.com/).

2. Click on the button "FREE TRIAL".

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarCRM/image002.png)

3. Fill the fields and  click on the button "Start Your Free Trial".  
Note. Use corporate e-mail, for example: tom@mycorp.com.

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarCRM/image004.png)

4. Then you will be redirected to the next page.

![image006](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarCRM/image006.png)

5. Go to your e-mail box and find the e-mail from "SugarCRM".

![image008](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarCRM/image008.jpg)

6. Click on the button "START FREE TRIAL".

![image010](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarCRM/image010.jpg)

7. After that you will be redirected to the trial website, login as ‘admin’.

![image012](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarCRM/image012.png)

8. Go to the profile and select "Administration".

![image014](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarCRM/image014.png)

9. In the next page click on the link "OAuth Keys".

![image016](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarCRM/image016.png)

10. Then create OAuth key.

![image018](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarCRM/image018.png)

11. Select OAuth version.

![image020](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarCRM/image020.png)

12. Fill all fields.

![image022](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarCRM/image022.png)

Copy your consumer Key and Secret key to your App, save your OAuth Key.

![image024](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/SugarCRM/image024.png)

Good Luck!
