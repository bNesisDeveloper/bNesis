# DHL Express App Registration (Public)  
Added 27.07.2018

For creating public APP you need to contact with (xmlrequests@dhl.com) along with your full Account details  like account number, address, region etc. 

DHL EXPRESS APP Registration (SANDBOX).
1. Go to: (https://xmlportal.dhl.com/login)
    And click on “Register Now”.

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/DHLExpress/image002.gif)

2. Fill all fields.

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/DHLExpress/image004.gif)

Note: In “Requestor Services” only checked “service” will worked:

![image006](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/DHLExpress/image006.jpg)

3. After click on “Save” you will be redirected to success page registration.

![image008](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/DHLExpress/image008.jpg)

4. Login to your account and click on the “XML Services Status”.

![image010](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/DHLExpress/image010.jpg)

5. Service status must be approved.  
Note. Service will be approved in 24 hours. If it still don’t be approved please contact DHL.

![image012](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/DHLExpress/image012.jpg)

6. Go to your e-mail (for example: protonmail).
Find “DHL XML Services Portal: Your Credentials”.

![image014](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/DHLExpress/image014.jpg)

7. Copy Site ID and Password to your app.

![image016](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/DHLExpress/image016.gif)

Good Luck.
