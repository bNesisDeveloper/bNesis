# Uber registration  
The description is made on 26/09/2018.

1. To register an account with Uber Ride, please go to (https://www.uber.com/).

2. Click the "Sign up to ride" button.

![image001](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Uber/image001.png)

3. In the form that opens, fill in the required fields and click on the "SIGN UP" button. In this way, you will register a new account.

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Uber/image002.png)

4. Download the mobile Uber application and log in to it. This way you will confirm your account.

5. To register the application in the Uber service, go to (https://developer.uber.com).

6. Press the "SIGN IN" button.

![image003](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Uber/image003.png)

7. Enter your registered e-mail or phone number and click on the "NEXT" button.

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Uber/image004.png)

8. Next, enter your password and click on the "NEXT" button.

![image005](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Uber/image005.png)

9. In the window that opens, click on the "register App" button.

![image006](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Uber/image006.png)

10. Specify the name of the application and make its description, accept the terms of use and click on the "Create" button.

![image007](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Uber/image007.png)

11. In the window that opens, specify ‘Redirect URI’ and ‘Privacy Policy URL’.

![image008](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Uber/image008.png)

12. Mark the permissions you need.

![image009](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Uber/image009.png)

13. At the bottom of the page click on the "SAVE" button.

![image010](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Uber/image010.png)

14. Now you can use the Client ID and Client secret provided to you to call the service API.

![image011](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Uber/image011.png)
