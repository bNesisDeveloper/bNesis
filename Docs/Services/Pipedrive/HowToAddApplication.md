¹ Pipedrive registration

1. First you need to create sandbox account. Go to: (https://pipedrive.readme.io/docs/developer-sandbox-account)  
And click on the link “webform”.

![image002[(https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Pipedrive/image002.gif)

2. Fill the form and press “Submit” button.

![image004[(https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Pipedrive/image004.gif)

3. Wait for a message to your e-mail from Pipedrive team.

![image006[(https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Pipedrive/image006.gif)

4. After activation click on profile in the right corner.

![image008[(https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Pipedrive/image008.gif)

5. Click on “Settings”.

![image010[(https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Pipedrive/image010.gif)

6. Click on “Marketplace Manager”.

![image012[(https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Pipedrive/image012.gif)

7. Click on the button “Create new app”.

![image014[(https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Pipedrive/image014.gif)

8. Fill the form and save app.

![image016[(https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Pipedrive/image016.gif)

9. Open your created app and find below “OAuth & Access scopes”,
Copy CliendId and ClientSecret to your app.

![image018[(https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Pipedrive/image018.gif)
