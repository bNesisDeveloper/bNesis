# Spotify Account & APP registration  
Added 27.07.2018.

1. Go to (https://www.spotify.com/)
and press button “Sign up”.

![image001](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image001.jpg)

2. Fill the fields or sign up with Facebook account.

![image002](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image002.jpg)

3. Then press “Sign up” button.

![image003](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image003.jpg)

4. After registration go to (https://developer.spotify.com/)
and click on “DASHBOARD”.

![image004](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image004.jpg)

5. Press “Log in” button.

![image005](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image005.jpg)

6. Read Spotify Developer Terms of Service. If you agree, press “Accept” button.

![image006](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image006.jpg)

7. Click on the “CREATE AN APP”.

![image007](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image007.jpg)

8. Fill the fields.

![image008](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image008.jpg)

9. Press a “Next” button.

![image009](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image009.jpg)

10. If developing for commercial integration press “Yes”,
if non-commercial press “NO” button.

![image010](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image010.jpg)

11. Press a “SUBMIT” button for creating Non-commercial app.

![image011](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image011.jpg)

12. After creating app you will be referenced to the app page.
Press “EDIT SETTINGS” button.

![image012](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image012.jpg)

13. Add redirect URL. For example: (http://localhost/api/authprovider/signin).

![image013](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image013.jpg)

14. Press a “SAVE” button.

![image014](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image014.jpg)

15. Copy Client ID and Client Secret to your app.

![image015](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bNesisDeveloper/bNesis/master/Docs/Services/Spotify/image015.jpg)

Good Luck!
